TITLE=How to Play

BASIC RULES:
Move one pawn in a straight line until it is blocked.
Then relocate one empty edge disc.
Place the removed disc so it touches at least 2 discs.
Win by connecting all of your pawns into one cluster.

CLASSIC:
Standard Nonaga rules.
Each player has 3 pawns.
Both players have a 5-minute timer.

CONTROL:
Special gold and silver discs appear on the board.
Landing on gold lets you move an enemy pawn.
Landing on silver lets you remove any empty disc.
Use power discs to essentially control space.

MEGA BOARD:
Played on a larger board.
Each player has 4 pawns instead of 3.
Win by connecting all 4 of your pawns.

SURVIVAL:
You always play as Red.
The AI plays as Blue.
You have 2 pawns while the AI has 3.
Survive for 15 turns before the AI connects its pawns.

CONTROLS:
R = Restart
X = Cancel selection
Esc = Open menu
Right-click enemy pawn = Preview enemy moves
